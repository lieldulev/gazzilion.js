What?

Gazzilion.js tries to solve the common problem of displaying the pretty or textual version for numbers.
Since it is in early stages expect API to break.

 

Methods: add_commas B billion K M million thousand to_letter to_word

 

Gazzilion.add_commas

Adds commas where needed. 


  
Gazzilion.B(number [, round = 0])

  
 Converts to the round short letter form (1B, 1,200B, 1.3B).
  

  
Gazzilion.billion(number [, round = 0])

  
 Converts to the round long word form.
  

  
Gazzilion.K(number [, round = 0])

  

    Converts to the round short letter form (1K, 1,200K, 1.3K). 
  

  

  
Gazzilion.M(number [, round = 0])

  
 Converts to the round short letter form (1M, 1,200M, 1.3M).
  

  
Gazzilion.million(number [, round = 0])

  
 Converts to the round long word form.
  

  
Gazzilion.thousand(number [, round = 0])

  
 Converts to the round long word form.
  

  
Gazzilion.to_letter(number [, round = 0])

  
 Converts to the round short letter form (1K, 1M, 1B).
  

  
Gazzilion.to_word(number [, round = 0])

  
 Converts to the round word form (1 thousand, 1 million, 1 billion).
